Best batting techniques for beginners?

By Kevin Thompson December 14, 2024

I'm new to cricket and want to improve my batting. What are the fundamental techniques I should focus on first? Any tips from experienced players would be greatly appreciated!

Michael Thompson December 14, 2024

Great question! Start with your stance - feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent. Focus on watching the ball closely and keeping your head still. Practice your forward and back defensive shots first before moving to attacking strokes.

Raj Patel December 14, 2024

I'd also recommend practicing in the nets regularly. Join our Tuesday evening practice sessions - we have coaches who can help you with the basics. The key is consistent practice and patience!

Equipment recommendations for new players

By Marcus Johnson December 8, 2024

For those just starting out, what essential equipment should they invest in first? Bat, pads, gloves, helmet - what's the priority order and any brand recommendations that offer good value for money?

Michael Thompson December 8, 2024

Safety first - helmet and pads are essential. A decent bat doesn't have to be expensive for beginners. Gray-Nicolls and Kookaburra offer good entry-level options. Our pro shop has some great deals for members!
