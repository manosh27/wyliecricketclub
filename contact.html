Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the club?

Contact us via the form above or visit our ground during practice sessions. We welcome players of all skill levels and offer trial sessions for new members.

What are the membership fees?

Annual membership is $150 for adults, $75 for students/seniors, and $50 for youth (under 18). This includes access to all facilities and coaching sessions.

Do you provide equipment?

We have basic equipment available for beginners, but regular players are encouraged to have their own gear. Our pro shop offers equipment at discounted prices for members.

When are practice sessions?

Regular practice sessions are held Tuesday and Thursday evenings (6-8 PM) for seniors, and Saturday mornings (9 AM-12 PM) for youth programs.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely! We have dedicated programs for beginners with professional coaching. Our friendly community welcomes players who are new to cricket.

Do you have women's cricket?

Yes! We have an active women's team that competes in the regional league. Practice sessions are held on Saturday mornings (9-11 AM).
