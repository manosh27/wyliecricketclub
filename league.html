League Rules & Regulations

Match Format

  • Premier Division: 40 overs per side
  • T20 Division: 20 overs per side
  • Powerplay: First 6 overs (T20), First 10 overs (40-over)
  • Maximum 8 overs per bowler (40-over), 4 overs (T20)

Playing Conditions

  • Minimum 11 players per team
  • Maximum 3 overseas players
  • Drinks break every 10 overs
  • DLS method for rain-affected matches

Points System

  • Win: 2 points
  • Tie/No Result: 1 point each
  • Loss: 0 points
  • Bonus points for batting/bowling performance

Equipment Standards

  • White clothing mandatory
  • Colored cricket balls (Red/White)
  • Protective gear required
  • ICC approved equipment only
