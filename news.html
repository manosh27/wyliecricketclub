News & Announcements

December 15, 2024

Season Opening Ceremony - Registration Now Open!

We are thrilled to announce the opening of the 2025 cricket season! Join us for our grand opening ceremony on January 15th, 2025, at the Wylie Cricket Ground.

The ceremony will feature:

  • Welcome speech by club president
  • Introduction of new coaching staff
  • Team registrations for all divisions
  • Exhibition matches
  • Food and refreshments for all attendees

Registration is now open for the following divisions:

  • Senior Division (18+): Competitive matches every weekend
  • Youth Division (12-17): Development focused with expert coaching
  • Junior Division (8-11): Fun-focused introduction to cricket
  • Women's Division: Promoting women's cricket in our community

Early bird registration is available until December 31st with a 20% discount on membership fees. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of Wylie's premier cricket community!

December 10, 2024

New Practice Facilities Unveiled

We're excited to announce the completion of our new state-of-the-art practice facilities. The new complex includes:

  • 6 professional-grade practice nets
  • Bowling machines for batting practice
  • Indoor training area for weather protection
  • Equipment storage and changing rooms

The facilities will be available to all registered members starting January 1st, 2025. Special training sessions will be conducted by our professional coaches.

December 5, 2024

Youth Cricket Program Launch

Introducing our comprehensive youth development program designed to nurture the next generation of cricket stars!

Program Highlights:

  • Professional coaching from former professional players
  • Age-appropriate skill development
  • Character building and teamwork focus
  • Pathway to competitive cricket

Ages 8-16 are welcome. Programs run every Saturday from 9 AM to 12 PM. Special introductory rates available for new participants.

November 28, 2024

Championship Victory Celebration

Congratulations to our senior team for winning the North Texas Cricket League Championship! After a thrilling final match against Dallas Cricket Club, our team emerged victorious by 45 runs.

Match Highlights:

  • Outstanding batting performance by captain Raj Patel (89 not out)
  • Excellent bowling figures by Ahmed Khan (5/42)
  • Brilliant fielding display throughout the match

The victory celebration will be held on December 20th at the clubhouse. All members and families are invited!

November 20, 2024

New Coaching Staff Announcements

We're pleased to welcome our new coaching staff for the 2025 season:

  • Head Coach: Michael Thompson (Former Texas State player)
  • Batting Coach: Priya Sharma (Former India U-19 player)
  • Bowling Coach: James Wilson (Former county cricket player)
  • Youth Coach: Sarah Martinez (Certified youth development specialist)

Our new coaching team brings over 50 years of combined experience and will help take our club to new heights.

November 15, 2024

Community Outreach Initiative

As part of our commitment to growing cricket in the community, we're launching several outreach programs:

  • School Visits: Introducing cricket to local schools
  • Free Clinics: Monthly free coaching sessions for newcomers
  • Equipment Drive: Collecting equipment for underprivileged youth
  • Community Matches: Fun matches open to the public

Volunteers are needed for these initiatives. Contact us if you'd like to help grow cricket in our community!

November 10, 2024

Annual General Meeting Recap

Our Annual General Meeting was held on November 8th with excellent attendance from members. Key decisions included:

  • Approval of budget for new facilities
  • Election of new committee members
  • Planning for 2025 season activities
  • Membership fee structure for next year

Thank you to all members who participated. Meeting minutes are available in the members' area of our website.
