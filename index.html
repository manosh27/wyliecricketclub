About Wylie Cricket Club

Established in 2015, Wylie Cricket Club has been the heart of cricket in Wylie, Texas. We pride ourselves on fostering a love for cricket while building a strong, inclusive community.

Our club welcomes players of all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting your cricket journey, you'll find a home with us.
